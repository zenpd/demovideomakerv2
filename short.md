Every day, financial institutions move trillions of dollars across SWIFT, A C H, RTGS, and SEPA. Treasury teams rely on spreadsheets and static rules to route payments. That's slow, inconsistent, and costly. Choosing the wrong rail wastes thousands of dollars per transaction.

## Agent Status Module
[url: http://host.containers.internal:5173]
[action: click]
[target: text=Agent Status]
[duration: 33]
The Agent Status module monitors AI agents responsible for different stages of the payment orchestration workflow. The Context Collector Agent gathers and enriches transaction data, the Policy Reasoner Agent performs compliance and regulatory validation, and the Optimizer Agent selects the best payment rail based on cost, speed, and reliability analysis. The dashboard also tracks each agent's uptime, health status, last activity check, and total tasks processed to ensure the entire orchestration system is operating efficiently.
